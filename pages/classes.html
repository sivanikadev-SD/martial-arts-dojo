FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need experience?

No! We welcome beginners of all fitness levels. Our instructors will guide you step-by-step.

What should I wear?

For your trial class, comfortable workout clothes are fine. We sell uniforms (Gi) at the dojo.

Is there a contract?

We offer flexible month-to-month options as well as discounted long-term tuitions.
